# Accessibility @ ECC

You should be developing your components using storybook as it _should_ automatically flag if there are any pressing accessibility issues for your component. Bear in mind while this will catch some issues it is recommended to also complete a round of manual accessibility testing to ensure our components are suitable for a wide range of people.
